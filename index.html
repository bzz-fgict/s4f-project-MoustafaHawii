Study organizer

This website was made for a school project. The website has two main functions at the moment. The first one is a Pomodoro Timer to keep track, how much you studied. It will show you when to take breaks, so you don't overwork yourself. The second function is a to-do list. In this to-do list, you will be able to add, delete and cross out your tasks.
